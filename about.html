Our Story

Hook and Go Logistics was founded in 2019 by Gibson Shambwe with a vision to provide specialized abnormal cargo transportation services in KwaZulu-Natal and across South Africa.

Starting with just a few trucks and a dedicated team, we have grown to become a trusted name in the logistics industry, known for our reliability, safety standards, and customer-focused approach.

Our headquarters is located at Unit 12, Runtown Way, Newlands West, KwaZulu-Natal 4037, where we coordinate all our operations and logistics services.

Our Mission

To provide safe, efficient, and cost-effective abnormal load transportation solutions while maintaining the highest standards of service and safety.

Our Vision

To be the leading abnormal load logistics provider in South Africa, recognized for excellence, reliability, and innovation in specialized transportation services.

Our Values

  • Safety: We prioritize the safety of our team, cargo, and other road users in all our operations.
  • Reliability: We deliver on our promises and ensure timely and secure transportation of your cargo.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service delivery.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical standards.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek innovative solutions to improve our services and operations.
